## What if your computer could think for itself?

Imagine having a group of helpful assistants living inside your computer. They're not just programs waiting for your commands — they have their own personalities, remember things you've told them, and can work together to get things done.

That's what we've built.

---

## What can they do for you?

### 🗓️ Never miss important dates

Ask them about any date in any calendar system:
- "When is Chinese New Year this year?"
- "When does Ramadan start?"
- "How many days until my anniversary?"

They understand over 30 different calendar systems from around the world.

### 💬 Chat with them anytime

Talk to them like you would chat with a friend. They remember your previous conversations, so you don't have to repeat yourself. Each has a distinct personality — some are more serious, others are more playful. You choose who you want to work with.

### 📝 Help with everyday tasks

Tell them what you need, and they'll figure out how to do it:
- "Read this file for me and summarize it"
- "Check which websites are available right now"
- "Send a message to my colleague"
- "Remind me to check email every Monday at 9 AM"

### ⏰ Set smart reminders

Not just regular alarms — they understand complex schedules:
- "Remind me on the 15th of every lunar month"
- "Remind me 3 days before any national holiday"
- "Check in with me every morning at 8 AM"

### 🤝 Work together as a team

Create multiple assistants with different specializations. One might be great at organizing your schedule, another at researching information, another at creative writing. They can even talk to each other and coordinate their work!

---

## How is this different from regular AI?

### They have memory

Most AIs forget everything after each conversation. These companions remember important things you tell them, building knowledge over time.

### They can take action

Instead of just giving you advice, they can actually **do things** — read files, browse the internet, send messages, create reminders, and more.

### They work while you sleep

Set them on tasks and they keep working even when you're away. Come back to find the work done.

### They have personality

Each companion is shaped by a "soul file" — think of it as their character sheet. You can create assistants with different communication styles, areas of expertise, and ways of working.

## What makes it special?

### Privacy-first

Everything runs on **your own computer**. Your conversations, memories, and data never leave your machine unless you explicitly ask them to access the internet. You're in full control.

### No subscription fees

This is open-source software. Free to use, modify, share with friends. No monthly fees, no premium tiers, no locked features.

### Grows with you

Start with a single simple assistant. As you discover what's possible, create more specialized companions. The system is designed to scale from casual use to serious productivity workflows.

### Built by a community

Developers from around the world are working to make it better. New features, calendar systems, language support, and capabilities are added regularly.

## Real-world examples

### Example 1: Planning a trip

**You**: "I'm going to Japan next month. Can you help me plan?"

**Your assistant**:
- Checks the Japanese calendar for holidays during your visit
- Researches weather forecasts
- Creates a day-by-day itinerary document
- Sets reminders for booking deadlines
- Shares the plan with your travel companions' assistants

### Example 2: Managing a project

**You**: "I need to finish this research paper by the end of the month."

**Your team**:
- **Research assistant**: Collects relevant sources and summarizes them
- **Writing partner**: Helps you structure arguments and refine prose
- **Schedule manager**: Sets milestones and sends progress reminders
- **Fact checker**: Verifies citations and cross-references information

They coordinate automatically, updating each other on progress.

### Example 3: Cultural calendar

**You**: "What are the important holidays this year?"

**Your assistant**:
- Checks Chinese Lunar Calendar for Spring Festival, Mid-Autumn Festival
- Looks up Islamic Calendar for Ramadan and Eid
- Finds Hebrew Calendar dates for High Holy Days
- Creates a unified calendar with all important dates
- Sets reminders one week before each event
